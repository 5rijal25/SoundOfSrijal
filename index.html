Ways to work together

Three paths, one steady guide

You don't need to know exactly what you need before reaching out. These overlap on purpose — most people start in one place and find they need a little of all three.

Healing From Past Trauma

Process difficult experiences in a private, judgment-free space, at a pace your nervous system can actually handle.

Career Clarity & Planning

Untangle what you actually want from your work, and leave with a realistic, written plan for getting there.

Ongoing Mentorship

Regular check-ins with one person who knows your story, for whenever life or work gets complicated again.

How it works

What to expect, from first message to first session

  1. 01

    Reach out

    Send a short message about what's bringing you here. No detail is too small or too heavy to share.

  2. 02

    A first conversation

    A relaxed introductory session to understand your situation and decide together whether this is a good fit.

  3. 03

    A plan, made with you

    Srijal proposes a simple path forward — healing-focused, career-focused, or both — and you adjust it until it feels right.

  4. 04

    Steady sessions

    Regular sessions, online or in person, with space to revisit the plan as you grow and your needs change.
